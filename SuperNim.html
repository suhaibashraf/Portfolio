About

I developed this game in python as a mini project for MSc module Theory and Practice of AI. MiniMax Algorithm is used for AI player

Unlike the traditional Nim game, It has stacks of stickes. There are two players. Player can split those stikes into two equal stacks, merge two same size of stacks and apply Collatz-Ulam, if sticks in a stack are odd. I spent 12 days for this project. Player can be Human or Computer (with AI or without AI)
